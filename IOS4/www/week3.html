  • What are the 'top selling' app categories in the U.S.?

    The most popular categories are games;social applications that range from taking photo's with a camera application that has neat filters which then allow you to share them on social networks, or social photo sharing applications that have a camera app mashed into thier social network.



  • What are the 'top selling' app categories in Japan?

    Games, utility applications, books, and photo applications.



  • What are the 'top selling' app categories in Great Britain?

    It pretty much the same as Japan, but games is the top selling.



  • Based on your three answers above, what might you infer about each of the markets listed?

    For the United States market, it is definitely all about games when it comes to the top selling applications.....users like to play games to kill time when they know that they have X amount of time to waste.
